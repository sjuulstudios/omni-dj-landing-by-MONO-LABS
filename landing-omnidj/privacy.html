Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data Omni DJ collects, how we use it, and what choices you have. Omni DJ is built by MONO LABS, based in the Netherlands. We follow EU GDPR.

1. What we collect

Omni DJ is local-first. Your audio files are processed entirely on your Mac and never uploaded to our servers.

The only data we collect:

  • Account info. Email address, hashed password, account creation date.
  • Subscription state. Active plan, billing status (handled by Stripe, we never see card details).
  • Clip metadata (optional). File names, durations, export ratios. Never the audio itself.
  • Crash reports (anonymous). If the app crashes, an anonymous report helps us fix bugs faster. You can opt out in Settings.

2. What we do not collect

  • Your DJ sets, audio files, or any media content.
  • Your unreleased music or work-in-progress recordings.
  • Browsing history, location, or IP address beyond the strictly necessary login session.

3. Where data lives

Account and subscription data are stored in Supabase (EU region) and Stripe. Both providers are GDPR-compliant data processors.

Your audio and exported clips live on your Mac. We have no copy.

4. How long we keep it

Account data: as long as your account exists. Delete your account in Settings to permanently remove your data within 30 days.

Crash reports: aggregated after 90 days, raw data deleted.

5. Your rights under GDPR

  • Right to access: ask what data we hold on you.
  • Right to rectification: correct any wrong data.
  • Right to erasure: request deletion of your account.
  • Right to data portability: export your account data.
  • Right to object: opt out of crash reporting.

Email omnidj@monohq-labs.com with any request and we respond within 30 days.

6. Cookies and tracking

The Omni DJ landing page uses Cloudflare Web Analytics, which is privacy-preserving and uses no cookies or fingerprinting. We do not run third-party trackers, advertising pixels, or marketing scripts.

7. Children

Omni DJ is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, the date at the top changes and active users get an email notification.

9. Contact

Data controller: MONO LABS, the Netherlands. Email omnidj@monohq-labs.com.
